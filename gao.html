Hung Yen's rice production potential

Hung Yen cultivates approximately 145,000-155,000 hectares of rice annually with a yield of 130-132 quintals/ha, stable output of nearly 1 million tons, meeting domestic demand and export requirements.

The province applies advanced techniques like alternate wetting and drying irrigation, UAV rice care, VietGAP and Organic standards, and has over 200 processing facilities with a capacity of 200,000 tons/year.

Key rice production areas

Hung Yen develops high-quality rice areas with main varieties like TBR39, Bac Thom 7 in Tien Hai and organic rice - silkworm ecological models in Dong Hung.

Districts like Quynh Phu, Vu Thu, Kien Xuong specialize in producing specialty rice varieties such as Tam Xuan, Hom Rau, Nep Keo, with many products achieving OCOP certification.

Modern technology in rice production

Hung Yen applies alternate wetting and drying techniques, uses UAVs for precise spraying and fertilizing, saving water, reducing 30% pesticide use, and improving production efficiency.

The province implements VietGAP, Organic models with water-nutrient sensor systems and over 200 processing facilities with 200,000 tons/year capacity to ensure rice quality.

BRAND DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY

1. Multimedia communication campaign

Organize the "Journey of Hung Yen Rice Grain" video contest (3-5 minutes) with English subtitles, introducing modern production processes and farmers' stories.

Develop brand slogans like "Hung Yen Rice - Purely Vietnamese. Globally Loved." for consistent use across media channels and product packaging.

2. Professional brand identity system

Design a unified identity system with the logo "Hung Yen Rice - Green Gold of Vietnam" combining rice ears and Red River imagery, green and yellow colors symbolizing sustainable development.

Develop premium packaging with VietGAP, Organic certifications and QR codes for traceability, helping consumers easily recognize and trust the products.

3. Digital platform for export support

Build a multilingual e-commerce website introducing Hung Yen rice brand, integrating online ordering and 24/7 customer support, optimized for mobile devices.

List products on international e-commerce platforms like Alibaba, Amazon to reach global importers and consumers.

4. "Faces of Hung Yen Rice" image campaign

Create artistic photo series in rice fields, processing plants and family kitchens to depict the journey from grain to delicious rice bowl.

Select diverse representatives including farmers, chefs, students and brand ambassadors to spread Hung Yen rice image to various target groups.

PROVINCIAL LEADERS' DIRECTIVES

Activities directing rice brand development

Provincial leaders regularly inspect production models, survey seed factories and rice fields, directing the application of technical advances to improve rice quality.

The province established a Rice Brand Development Steering Committee, building a comprehensive plan from production to consumption and promoting trade.
