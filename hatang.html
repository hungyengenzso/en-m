NEW HUNG YEN ECONOMIC INFRASTRUCTURE

New Hung Yen - formed from the merger of Hung Yen and Thai Binh - is strongly developing its economic infrastructure system

Transportation System

• Hung Yen has 60.9 km of modern highways, connecting with important traffic routes, enabling fast movement between economic centers and industrial zones.

• 151 km of national highways in the area have been upgraded and expanded, supporting goods transportation, agricultural products and promoting commercial activities in the province.

• 13 large bridges across rivers play a role in connecting areas within and outside the province.

Port System

• Hung Yen has 11 large ports serving waterway cargo transportation, helping reduce road traffic pressure and supporting regional economic circulation.

• The province owns modern container ports, meeting large-scale cargo loading and transportation needs, with convenient connections to logistics centers.

• 15 industrial wharves have been built to serve production and import-export activities.

Industrial Infrastructure

• Hung Yen currently has 7 concentrated industrial zones, planned modernly, attracting many domestic and foreign investors, creating momentum for local economic development.

• 15 medium and small industrial clusters have been built throughout districts, supporting production development.

• The province also owns a 500-hectare high-tech park, aiming to attract advanced technology projects and promote economic restructuring towards modernization.

KEY PROJECTS

1. Hung Yen - Thai Binh Expressway

• The Hung Yen - Thai Binh expressway is about 45 km long with 6 lanes, ensuring modern technical standards for high-speed traffic.

• This route connects directly with the Hanoi - Hai Phong expressway, helping to shorten travel time and enhance trade in the Red River Delta region.

• The project has a total investment of 12,000 billion VND, being one of the key infrastructure works.

2. Thai Hung Bridge

• Thai Hung Bridge crosses the Red River with a length of about 3.2 km, modern design ensures safe and smooth traffic year-round.

• The project plays an important role in directly connecting Hung Yen and Thai Binh, contributing to promoting trade and regional economic development.

• The project has a total investment of about 3,500 billion VND.

3. Eco-Industrial Park

• The 300-hectare eco-industrial park in Tien Lu is synchronously planned, creating production development space associated with environmental protection.

• This area prioritizes attracting clean industrial projects, high technology, limiting negative impacts on local ecosystems.

• The centralized wastewater treatment system is built modernly, ensuring safety standards.
