SPIRITUAL TOUR INTRODUCTION

The Red River spiritual tourism route is a cultural-spiritual journey connecting the "sacred lands of talented people" along the mother river of Vietnam's wet rice civilization

CULTURAL AND SPIRITUAL SIGNIFICANCE

The Red River spiritual tourism route holds profound cultural significance, connecting historical relics and ancient temples along the majestic Red River. This journey is not just a trip but also an opportunity for visitors to learn about the history, culture and beliefs of the Vietnamese people.

  • Preserving, maintaining and promoting traditional spiritual cultural values
  • Connecting sacred lands bearing the mark of "sacred lands of talented people"
  • Opportunity to explore spiritual depth and the moral of "remembering the source when drinking water"

HISTORICAL SIGNIFICANCE - NATIONAL IDENTITY

  • Journey through major cultural regions of the Red River delta
  • Awakening national pride, affirming Vietnamese cultural identity
  • Educating the young generation about heritage preservation
  • Recreating the development history of the Northern Delta region
  • Connecting important historical relics from the Hung Kings period to present

ECONOMIC AND SOCIAL SIGNIFICANCE

  • Inter-regional tourism linkage model between localities
  • Creating jobs for local people
  • Calling for private investment under PPP model
  • Developing synchronized tourism infrastructure
  • Promoting Vietnamese cultural image to the world

UNIQUE CULTURAL HERITAGE

The Red River spiritual journey is a living museum of Vietnamese culture, preserving spiritual values over thousands of years of history. From Mother Goddess worship, Saint worship to unique folk festivals, each destination carries its own story of history and culture.

  • Mother Goddess worship - UNESCO intangible cultural heritage
  • Unique mediumship rituals, Chau Van singing
  • Dragon dance, unique folk performances
  • Ancient temple architecture hundreds of years old

Red River - Sacred flow

The mother river that deposits alluvium and crystallizes the spiritual, religious and unique folk cultural values of the Vietnamese people.

Highlight destinations

Tips to help you have the most fulfilling spiritual pilgrimage

SPIRITUAL TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

A spiritual trip is not just a journey of discovery but also an opportunity to purify the soul. To have a meaningful and smooth trip, visitors should note the following important points.

Clothing when visiting temples should be discreet, polite, preferably in elegant colors. Bring small change for offerings, but avoid excessive burning of votive papers. When entering temple areas, keep quiet, don't speak loudly, don't point at worship statues. Learn about local customs and rituals in advance to avoid violating taboos.

Ideal time

Spring (Jan-Mar)

Season of major festivals, cool weather, many unique cultural activities

Summer (Apr-Jun)

View golden rice fields, warm weather, convenient for travel

Autumn (Sep-Nov)

Cool climate, beautiful natural scenery, less rain

Preparation

  • Discreet, polite clothing (preferably dark colors)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Small change for offerings (exchange in advance)
  • Camera or phone (silent mode in temples)
  • Umbrella/raincoat for rain or sun
  • Personal medicine (if needed)

Specialties

  • Lang Kenh steamed rice rolls (North Hung Yen)
  • Quynh Coi fish soup (South Hung Yen)
  • Ninh Binh burnt rice
  • Ne Chau black sticky rice cake
  • Me So sweetened sticky rice
