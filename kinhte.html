Hung Yen launches three projects to celebrate National Day (Sept 2)

    Provincial General Hospital Project

  • Built on 12 hectares at Thai Binh Medical Center
  • Modern design, 1,200 beds with capacity to expand to 1,500
  • Total investment: about VND 3,700 billion (excluding equipment costs)

    Red River Cultural Heritage, Tourism & Economic Corridor

  • Category A project with funding close to VND 9,300 billion
  • 56 km road linking Van Giang, Khoai Chau, Kim Dong and Hung Yen City
  • Starts at Xuan Quan (Van Giang) near Hanoi & Ecopark; ends at Tan Hung (Hung Yen City)
  • Expected to connect key spiritual-cultural hubs: Thang Long – Pho Hien – Tam Chuc – Bai Dinh – Perfume Pagoda

    Affordable Housing Project in Yen My II Industrial Park

  • Location: Trung Hoa commune and Yen My town, spanning 30.9 ha
  • Investment: over VND 4,960 billion
  • Scale: 9,000 social housing units, up to 11 floors, total floor area nearly 580,000 m²
  • Designed to accommodate about 11,500 residents, primarily workers in and around Yen My II Industrial Park
